About Las Cookie

Las Cookie is a family-owned bakery specializing in handcrafted cookies made with the finest ingredients. Since 1995, we have been delighting customers with our unique flavors and warm, freshly baked treats. Our mission is to spread happiness, one cookie at a time!

3 Magic Steps to Get Your Magic Cookie

  1. Choose Your Favorite: Browse our delicious cookie cards and pick your favorite flavor.
  2. Place Your Order: Visit our outlet or call us to place your order for fresh, handcrafted cookies.
  3. Enjoy the Magic: Savor the taste and share the happiness with friends and family!
